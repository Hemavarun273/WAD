Profile

Jojo's Bizzare Adventure was created by the Manga artist Hirohiko Araki in the year 1987.

Manga author Hirohiko Araki is practically synonymous with his longest and greatest work, JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, but there is much more to know about Araki than the running joke about his possible vampire status or "Araki forgot" memes. Hirohiko Araki is a fascinating and inspirational author for anyone who learns more about him and discovers how he operates
